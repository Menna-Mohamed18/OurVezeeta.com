what is mental health?

Signs of mental health issues vary but may include persistent sadness, extreme mood swings, withdrawal from social activities, changes in sleep or appetite, difficulty concentrating, excessive worries, or substance abuse.

How do I know if I need professional help for my mental health concerns?

Seeking professional help is advisable if your emotional or psychological struggles interfere with daily life, work, relationships, or if you experience intense or prolonged distress that you can't manage on your own.

What are some effective ways to improve mental health?

Engaging in regular exercise, practicing relaxation techniques like mindfulness or meditation, maintaining a balanced diet, getting enough sleep, seeking social support, and considering therapy or counseling can help improve mental health.

What is the difference between a psychologist, psychiatrist, therapist, and counselor?

Psychologists hold advanced degrees in psychology and offer counseling or psychotherapy. Psychiatrists are medical doctors who can prescribe medications and provide therapy. Therapists and counselors are trained professionals who offer talk therapy to help manage mental health issues.
