Know more about Psychiatry
What is Psychiatry specialty?

Psychiatry is the specialty responsible for diagnosing, treating, and preventing psychological, emotional, and behavioral disorders.

What diseases does a psychiatrist treat?
  • Depression
  • Epilepsy
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)
  • Addiction
What is the treatment of OCD?

Treating obsessive-compulsive disorder usually involves several options, including:

  • Antidepressants and anti-anxiety medications.
  • Psychological and behavioral therapy, which includes anxiety control training, exposure to fears, and cognitive control.
  • Electroconvulsive therapy, such as electroshock therapy and intensive magnetic frequency therapy.
  • Cognitive-behavioral therapy, which focuses on positive thinking and changing negative thoughts.
